Information Sharing Statement

De-identified derived data and code underlying this study are publicly available at [REPOSITORY URL] (DOI: [DOI]). The repository includes the 10 × 75 derived feature matrix, the 10 × 73 ketone-excluded feature matrix, the long-format audit table, data dictionaries, PCA scores, PCA loadings, a public reproduction script, a fully synthetic example input file, and supplementary rank-based PCA sensitivity outputs. These deposited materials reproduce the reported numerical results and repository outputs from the deposited audit table. Publicly released materials omit exact participant ages, and nonessential participant-level duplicate output files were removed, to reduce re-identification risk in this small cohort. Raw clinical OAT reports are not included because they contain human-subject information and proprietary laboratory formatting. Public release of the deposited materials was conducted in accordance with The University of Mississippi Institutional Review Board protocol 24-007 and institutional policy.
